Tocks Study Technique!



The Tocks Study system is a powerful method of studying that is useful in eliminating distractions. Students will study in 45 minute chunks, and will use the notepad feature to write down any distractions/distracting thoughts. That way, you don’t have to “juggle all the other things your brain wants to think about.” At the end of the 45 minute study session, you will earn 2 academic study points!
