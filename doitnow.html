Do It Now Method!



The Do It Now Method is a productivity technique where you immediately choose which task you are going to work on. In order to best use our resources, we recommend you list out all your tasks in the Get it Done page so you have a clear idea of what you need to do. Press on the show to-do list button and use the 30 second timer below to choose a task to complete right away. Press the complete button when you are done with each task to earn 2 points.
