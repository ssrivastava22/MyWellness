My Wellness


My Wellness is a website that promotes mental health care among youth with an emphasis on education. Students are able to study using multiple time management techniques while also being able to care for their mental health. Our website provides a mental health track with 4 different mental health care activities and a studying track with 4 different study methods.
My Wellness uses a point system where students can receive a mental health point for every mental health care activity they do or a study point for every study session they complete. By setting daily goals to reach a certain amount of points, they can keep track of their studying progress while also ensuring that they are receiving the necessary care for their mental health. In addition, they can utilize our journal page to reflect on their day which can help reorganize thoughts and be helpful for their overall wellbeing.
