Pomodoro Study Technique

The Pomodoro Method is a productivity technique where you work for 25 minutes and take a five minute break. Each of these 25-minute periods is called a “Pomodoro”. It is highly suggested that you complete a mental health activity during the break. You will be awarded 1 study point for every session completed.
