Academic Points:


The Get Things Done Method is to list out everything that needs to be done and break down bigger tasks into smaller actionable items. There are 2 ways you can use our resources. You may use the Get Things Done Method and press complete to be awarded 1 study point or you can save your list and press the button "Go to Do It Now" in order to combine the Do It Now and Get Things Done study techniques. Your list that you saved from this page will be transferred to the Do it Now page. Use the following format:

big task:

- smaller actionable item

- smaller actionable item
